Yet another rainy day

Over the northern and central areas of New Zealand more rainfall falls in winter than in summer, dramatically increasing the amount of wet days per month, whereas for much of the southern part of New Zealand, winter is the season of least rainfall and the amount of wet days per month remain relatively constant throughout the year.
